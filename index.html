📚 Rules

Two Player Goal: First player to control 4 Hour Territories with at least 1 Late-Hour capture wins.

One Player Goal: Capture the required number of territories before Clock Stability reaches 0.

Battle Moves

  • Harvest Minute: Type how many minute ticks the long hand has traveled.
  • Countdown Dash: Type minutes remaining until next hour.
  • Capture Hour: Choose the active territory.
  • Quarter Power: At :15 or :45, choose the correct quarter phrase and hour.
  • Challenge / Cleanse: Attack an opponent territory in two-player, or cleanse corruption in solo.
  • Fortify / Rescue: Shield a captured territory in two-player, or rescue a threatened hour in solo.
