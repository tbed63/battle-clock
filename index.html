How to Play Battle Clock!

Goal: Be the first player to control 4 Hour Territories. At least one of your captures must happen during a Late Hour time, such as 5:48, 7:53, or 2:59.

The Big Clock Idea

The clock is the battlefield. The short hand shows the current Hour Territory. The long hand shows how many minute ticks have passed since the top of the hour.

Example: at 5:58, the short hand is very close to 6, but the active territory is still 5. The 6 Territory opens only when the minute hand reaches the top.

On Your Turn

  1. Look at the analog clock and the current battle time.
  2. Decide how to use the time strategically.
  3. Choose one battle move.

Battle Moves

  • Harvest Energy: Gain energy. Late-hour times and exact non-five-minute times can give extra energy.
  • Capture Current Hour: Spend 3 energy to capture the active Hour Territory.
  • Challenge Territory: Spend 2 energy to knock an opponent's active territory back to open.
  • Block Next Hour: During Late Hour times only, spend 1 energy to temporarily lock the upcoming territory.
  • Pass Turn: Skip your move and give the turn to the other player.

Late Hour Rule

A Late Hour time is any time from :45 to :59. These are powerful because the next hour looks close, but it is not open yet.

Winning

Control 4 Hour Territories, including at least 1 Late-Hour Capture.
