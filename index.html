How to Play Battle Clock!

Goal: Be the first player to control 4 Hour Territories. At least one capture must happen during a Late Hour time, such as 5:48, 7:53, or 2:59.

The Big Clock Idea

The time is hidden while you make your move. You have to read the analog clock to power your strategy.

At 5:58, the short hand is close to 6, but the active territory is still 5. The 6 Territory opens only when the minute hand reaches the top.

Battle Moves

  • Quarter Power Up: If the clock shows :15 or :45, choose the correct quarter phrase and hour. Correct answers add 25% of your current energy. If you try it when the clock is not :15 or :45, you lose 25% of your current energy. This bonus does not use up your regular turn.
  • Harvest Minute Energy: Type how many minute ticks the long hand has traveled. The farther after the hour, the more energy you earn.
  • Capture Current Hour: Choose the active Hour Territory. Spend 1500 energy to capture it.
  • Challenge Territory: Choose the active Hour Territory. If your opponent owns it, spend 2200 energy to attack it. If it is fortified, the shield breaks but the opponent keeps the territory.
  • Fortify Territory: Choose the active Hour Territory. If you control it, spend 1000 energy to protect it from one successful challenge.
  • Block Next Gate: Choose the next hour that is about to open. Spend 600 energy. Works only during late-hour times.
  • Countdown Dash: Type how many minutes remain until the next hour opens. This is harder, so it pays about 1.5 times as much as Harvest Minute Energy.

Wrong Answers

Wrong answers show the correct answer and why. The explanation stays on screen until the player clicks Continue.
