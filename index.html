📖 Rules

Goal: Be the first player to control 4 Hour Territories — with at least 1 Late-Hour capture (at :45 or later).

The Big Clock Idea

The digital time is hidden while you move. Read the analog clock to power your strategy!

At 5:58, the hour hand is near 6, but the active territory is still 5. Territory 6 only opens when the minute hand reaches the top.

Battle Moves

  • ⚡ Quarter Power Up: If the clock shows :15 or :45, pick the correct phrase and hour. Correct = +25% energy. Wrong timing = −25% energy. Doesn't use your regular turn.
  • 🌿 Harvest Minute Energy: Type how many minute ticks the long hand has traveled from the top. More ticks = more energy.
  • 💨 Countdown Dash: Type how many minutes remain until the next hour opens. Pays ~1.5× more than Harvest.
  • 🏴 Capture Current Hour: Choose the active Hour Territory. Costs 1500 energy.
  • ⚔️ Challenge Territory: Choose the active territory your opponent owns. Costs 2200 energy. Breaks a shield or removes control.
  • 🛡️ Fortify Territory: Choose your active territory. Costs 1000 energy. Protects against one successful Challenge.

Wrong Answers

Wrong answers reveal the correct time and explain why. The screen stays until you click Continue.
